Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, and local storage. This Cookie Policy explains how BuilderRates Financial Services, LLC (“BuilderRates,” “we,” “us”) uses these technologies on our Site and how you can control them. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

2. Technologies we use

Strictly necessary. These cookies are required for the Site to function, such as security, load balancing, and remembering cookie preferences where we offer a choice.

Analytics and performance. We may use first- or third-party analytics tools to understand how visitors use the Site (for example, pages viewed and general geography). Where configured, Google Tag Manager may load tags that set cookies on behalf of analytics or advertising vendors.

Functional. We may use technologies that remember preferences you select during your visit.

3. Third parties

Third-party providers may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies. We do not control those technologies. Common categories include analytics (e.g., Google Analytics if enabled through our tag manager) and embedded content.

4. How long cookies last

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for a defined period or until you delete them. Retention periods vary by cookie; we configure vendors consistent with our agreements and operational needs.

5. Your choices

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through settings. Blocking all cookies may affect Site functionality. You may also use industry opt-out tools where available for interest-based advertising. For more on how we use personal data in connection with cookies, see our Privacy Policy.

6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or law. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates the latest revision.
