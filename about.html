About BuilderRates

Our mission

Lazarus LLC (“BuilderRates”) was formed to help serious real estate investors spend less time chasing capital and more time executing deals. We operate an introduction and marketing platform that connects borrowers and sponsors with institutional lenders and specialized programs suited to construction, bridge, rental, and portfolio strategies.

We are compensated by lending and referral partners when we deliver qualified opportunities—we do not charge borrowers a separate placement fee for our core matchmaking service. Our incentives are aligned with surfacing durable financing options, not padding margin on a single product.

Where we operate

Our team works with investors and properties across the United States, subject to lender geography and eligibility. Our principal office is in Sheridan, Wyoming; lender partners may operate under their own licenses and credit policies.

Leadership
