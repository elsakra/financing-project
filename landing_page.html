Capital Intelligence FAQ

How is this free for me? expand_more
We are compensated by our lender partners for bringing them high-quality, pre-vetted deals. Our service is 100% free for the investor, and we never add extra fees on top of the lender's quote.
Do I have to use the lender you recommend? expand_more
No. You are under no obligation to use any of our recommendations. We provide the data and the options; the final decision is always yours.
Will this impact my credit score? expand_more
Initial analysis and preliminary term sheets are generated using a soft credit pull which does not impact your score. Only when you proceed to a full application with a specific lender will a hard pull occur.
How fast can I get funded? expand_more
Term sheets are usually delivered within 48 hours. Depending on the product (e.g., Fix & Flip), funding can occur in as little as 7-10 business days.
What areas do you cover? expand_more
We facilitate financing for residential and commercial investment properties in all 50 U.S. states.
