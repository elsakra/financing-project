Security

Lazarus LLC (“BuilderRates”) takes reasonable steps to protect information submitted through our Services. This page summarizes our high-level practices. It is not an exhaustive technical specification and does not modify our contracts or regulatory obligations.

Transport and hosting

Our public website is delivered over HTTPS (TLS). We use reputable hosting and infrastructure providers that maintain industry-standard physical and network controls. Data may be processed in the United States.

Access controls

Access to non-public systems and borrower information is limited to personnel and subprocessors with a business need. We use authentication, logging, and least-privilege principles appropriate to our size and risk profile.

Vulnerability reporting

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in our website or services, please contact us at hello@builderrates.com with a clear description and, if possible, steps to reproduce. We ask that you allow reasonable time for us to investigate before public disclosure. You may also reference our security contact file at /.well-known/security.txt.

Limitations

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of passwords and credentials for third-party services.
